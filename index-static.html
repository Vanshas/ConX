F.A.Q

  • Where and when will CON-X be held?
    It will take place on May 7 and starting at 8:30am in Palma de Mallorca.
  • How will the event take place?
    Upon your arrival, a TravelgateX volunteer will register you and take you to the coffee spot to enjoy a delicious breakfast. Meanwhile, a member of our team will walk you through the building and introduce you to others. Every attendee will be welcomed in this fun environment to help them start fluid conversations with other guests.
  • Once seated and enjoying the atmosphere, we will take a few minutes to answer all of your questions and explain the approach of the event.
  • Then the first speakers will start telling their success stories to inspire entrepreneurs and help you understand how they’ve succeeded. After each talk, a 10-minute break will be allocated to answer your questions and doubts.
  • Right after a lunch break, we will seat down alongside other members to create individual groups. During these minutes, a facilitator will go over tactical, specific and interactive topics with you.
  • Finally, we will wrap up the day with an afternoon party for you to take full advantage of the event and meet more people in a more laid-back atmosphere.
  • What will I get with my ticket?
    Each ticket includes breakfast, refreshments, lunch, conference attendance, worldwide networking and after party.
  • How many tickets are for sale?
    The entry is limited but, we will have up to 400 tickets for sale. Make sure to grab yours quickly if you wish to attend the CON-X.
